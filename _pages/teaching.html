> To teach is not to transfer knowledge but to create the > possibilities for the production or construction of knowledge. > > - Paulo Freire I see teaching in the dynamic Information Age as a collective activity, or dialogue between participants. My objectives as a university teacher are to develop enriched environments that promote active learning, challenge participants with modern methods and technologies that foster innovation, and empower students with evidence-based practices to facilitate their learning as a life-long endeavor. I see student fluency in contemporary technologies and media as a major asset in the classroom, and encourage students to build from those strengths. I also encourage students to embrace "making mistakes" as a core learning tenet, whether practising coding or composition.
